Temptation

Tue Jun 2, 2009
personal / abstract / poetry

Temptation is haunting my head It’s keeping me a stranger to my bed Tormenting me like a nightmare It’s making the moment hard to bare

I have this grip in my fingers and a smell that lingers I have this taste on my tongue and mind blowing song

Temptation is all around It’s waiting to be found Dwelling inside and showing It’s crawling and crawling
