TheDontAskWhyCollection: something with lines

Fri Apr 14, 2006
abstract

I just did something with lines a few days ago.. Drawing some easter decorations for a website on some paper, scanned it in and just went over the lines using Gimp and the Pencil there..

Without thinking, I just did it.. And the outcome was pretty nice and I liked it. Today while making my own site, SomeAbstractTypeShit, I took my picture you see in the corner and ‘blacklined’ it.

But don’t ask me why! Hence the name of the abstract typed shit I’m currently producing ‘TheDontAskWhyCollection’. It’s my stuff, I don’t bother whether people like it, but I’m just showing it anyway (and secretly hoping somebody likes it and pays me millions.. HAHAHA..).

Here is the first one:

http://www.some-abstract-type-shit.com/sleeping_dog_in_pompeii.html

I’m using pictures I took. More to come. I think. Maybe I get bored of it and will make other things.
