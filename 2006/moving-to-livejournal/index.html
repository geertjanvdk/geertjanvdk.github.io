Moving to LiveJournal

Thu Apr 13, 2006
noise

I got a bit tired of always wanting to develop my own software all the time. First I never get to it, second I’m never happy with it, third: I need more free time!

So, I decided to give blogger.com a try first. It looked nice! But, I don’t know.. LiveJournal got me more interested. I think the layout of my blog could use some more better design here and there. I will see to that.

All my entries from my old ‘blog’ have been back-posted to LiveJournal. As one can see, I am not a regular blogger, but I think I’ll get more into it now. Maybe I just happen to have more shit to stay.

The ‘Some Abstract Type Shit’ comes from an album from the great Mo’Wax label. Look it up!
