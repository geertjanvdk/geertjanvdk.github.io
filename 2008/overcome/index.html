Overcome

Tue Dec 30, 2008
personal / abstract / poetry

It’s coming back that is hard Back to where one grew up had his first kiss and beer That going back is heavy on the heart heavy like a stone you once got up you threw and happy it was just near Memories are haunting or revive what you have done I have the images, the feelings, that I have to overcome.
