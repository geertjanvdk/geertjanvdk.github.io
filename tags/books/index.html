Although I adore books, I’m a casual reader. I’m also a slow reader, but Dan Brown’s Angels and Demons was like a train ride: the stops between were annoying! And it was a nice read! The murders, plots, the rush through Rome visiting it’s finest art.. it was not easy to put the book down. Although I was suspecting the chamberlain pretty early, it ended with some nice twist and surprises.
