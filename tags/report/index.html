Krak&oacute;w and Poland preparing for next big flooding

Tue May 18, 2010
photography / report

Today I walked along the Wisła (Vistula river) in Kraków. The past week it didn’t stop raining and it starts to show. It is the first time that I personally see such event. The river seems to be taking over the city. The Wavel castle is still safe standing up the hill. The Dragon on the other hand is suffering from lack of attention as all eyes and cameras are now on the Vistula river.
