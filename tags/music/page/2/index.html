Sophia!

Tue Dec 5, 2006
music

Ah, I like Sophia!! Have no fear, it ain’t a girl. It’s a band! Last year I heard them live from the AB (Brussels) and bought immediately the Album on iTunes. Now with the second album Technology won't save us it’s again a hit. I’m happy Robin Proper-Sheppard didn’t stop his Sophia-project after People are like seasons. I’m usually very picky about music, but Sophia just hits the spot. Don’t know, it hugs my neo-grunge dark brainwaves.
